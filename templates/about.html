ABOUT US

Youth Alive Kenya is a non-governmental organization that was founded in 1999 by a group of young Kenyans who were passionate about promoting youth development and community engagement. The organization is run by young people for young people, and its mission is to empower young people to become leaders and agents of positive change in their communities. The organization works with young people across Kenya to provide opportunities for personal and professional development, including mentorship, leadership training, and community service projects. We have also implemented several initiatives to promote health and well-being among young people, including programs focused on sexual and reproductive health, drug abuse prevention, and mental health awareness(Kirori & Karanja, 2019). Over the years, we have received support from various partners, including the United Nations Development Programme, UNICEF, and the Kenyan government. The organization has also won several awards and accolades for its work in promoting youth development and community engagement.

MISSION

To impact the lives of thousands of young people across the country and commit to empower young people to become leaders and agents of positive change.

VISION

Be a leading voice for youth empowerment and community engagement
